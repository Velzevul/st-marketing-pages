Who we are

StudyTube is a VC backed online education startup in the center of Amsterdam.

While e-learning has been around for many years, it hasn’t really taken off yet. We feel that now is the time and we’re hell-bent on making it happen!

In order to do so we rely on

  • A young, super smart and culturally diverse team, working together closely in our HQ in Amsterdam.
  • An intense focus on design and usability that makes us stand out.
  • A get-things-done attitude that challenges each team member to over-deliver every day while allowing the freedom to do so in the way that is most productive for each individual.

Having found product market fit, we are now growing the team in three areas - sales, education and development.

What do we offer

A framework for:

  • Experts to design smart online courses with video materials and tests. The courses are licensed and the experts can sell them individually as well as offer them to training institutes.
  • Training institutes to create the perfect course quickly and easily from our online course-library. They can use the materials in our library, but also materials from their own experts.
  • Student to take courses that are intuitive and highly user friendly. The student can pick when and where he studies him or herself.
  • Companies to get customized courses that are cost efficient and suit their training demand. Furthermore they have access to their employees learning progress.
